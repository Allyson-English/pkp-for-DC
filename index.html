Core Issues

Environment



Our neighborhood is rapidly developing with new construction and businesses. I want to ensure that the new buildings going up are at the forefront of environmental sustainability and that we devote adequate resources to incorporating green practices within existing infrastructure.

Affordable Housing



DC is already an expensive place to live, and costs are continuing to rise, pushing existing residents and vulnerable populations out and keeping new residents from coming in. As ANC Commissioner I want to take steps to ensure that affordable housing is accessible in our community.

Statehood



This is an issue that we need to continue to raise at every level of government. DC Statehood not only means federal representation for DC residents, but also unlocks significant access to federal resources we could use to create positive change for DC residents across our city.

What is the ANC?

Washington, DC is divided into eight different wards that are each represented by a member of the city council. Each ward is further subdivided into Advisory Neighborhood Commissions. If you live in the Mount Vernon area, like I do, you are most likely part of Ward 6, ANC 6E. Each ANC is further divided into “Single Member Districts,” which each elect one Commissioner to represent them at the ANC. If you live in district 6E05, I am running to represent you.
